Plugins

Plugins

Tasks

Harpoon task plugins are a great way to share simple shell helpers with your peers, or integrate full-blown CLI apps into your Harpoon environment.

Services

Harpoon service plugins enable you to share Docker Compose configurations between multiple projects. For example, you may be building a multi-service (or microservice) architecture comprised of services that all use MySQL. Instead of running multiple instances of MySQL, consuming valuable workstation resources, you can use Harpoon to expose and manage a single instance that each of your services may use.
